WDD 131: Website Planning Document

Site Name

Midlands Chamber of Commerce
This name represents a professional organization supporting businesses in Zimbabwe’s Midlands region. It aligns with the purpose of promoting local commerce, events, and community partnerships.

Optional domain availability: midlandschamber.org

Site Purpose

The Midlands Chamber of Commerce website will serve as a platform for connecting local businesses and communities. It will feature member directories, upcoming events, current weather, a spotlight for featured businesses, and provide a call-to-action for joining the Chamber.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events can help me network with other business owners in the Midlands area?
  • How do I apply to join the Midlands Chamber of Commerce?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #0a3d62 - Used for headings, CTA buttons, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #3c6382 - Used in navigation backgrounds and hover states.
  • Accent Color: #1b4f72 - Used for borders and emphasis.
  • Highlight Color: #d9d9ec - Used for background of alternating sections.

Typography

  • Headings: Montserrat, sans-serif — Used for titles, headers, and navigation.
  • Body Text: Open Sans, sans-serif — Used for paragraphs and general content.
